Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the MateDrive app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Inhong Kim (hereby referred to as "Service Provider").

How the Application Works

MateDrive is a companion app for TeslaMate, a self-hosted data logger for Tesla vehicles. The Application connects only to a TeslaMate server that you configure yourself, and displays vehicle data such as driving history, charging records, and vehicle status.

Information Collection and Use

The Application does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information.

  • No data is sent to the developer or any third-party server
  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics are gathered
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used

The Application communicates solely with the TeslaMate server you provide. All data retrieved is cached locally on your device for performance purposes and is never shared externally.

Third Party Access

The Application does not share any information with third parties. The Application does not connect to Tesla servers directly — all data is retrieved from your personal TeslaMate installation.

Opt-Out Rights

Since no data collection occurs, there is no data collection to opt out of. You may uninstall the Application at any time using your device's standard uninstall process.

Data Retention Policy

No user data is retained by the Service Provider. Any data cached by the Application exists only locally on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the Application.

Children

The Application is suitable for users of all ages and does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Security

Since no data leaves your device to the developer, your information is as secure as your device and your TeslaMate server. We recommend keeping your device's operating system up to date with the latest security patches and securing your TeslaMate server appropriately.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. The Service Provider will notify you of any changes to the Privacy Policy by updating this page with the new Privacy Policy. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes.
