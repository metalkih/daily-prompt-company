Key Features

Everything You Need for Your Tesla

Browse your driving and charging data with beautiful visualizations, all from your self-hosted TeslaMate server.

dashboard

Live Dashboard

Real-time battery level, estimated range, temperature, tire pressure, Sentry Mode status, and current location.

route

Drive History

Complete driving logs with route maps, energy consumption, efficiency metrics, and interactive speed/power/elevation charts.

bolt

Charge Tracking

Detailed charging session records with energy added, cost, battery progression charts, and DC fast / AC charger detection.

explore

Trip Management

Group related drives into trips, discover frequent route patterns, and view total distance and energy summaries.

How It Works

Simple Setup, Instant Access

1

Host TeslaMate

Run your own TeslaMate instance. It logs your Tesla data to a self-hosted database you control.

2

Connect MateDrive

Enter your TeslaMate server URL in the app. MateDrive connects directly — no third-party servers involved.

3

Explore Your Data

Browse drives, charges, trips, and live dashboard — all beautifully presented on your iPhone.
